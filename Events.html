Description

At Olin many events hosted by clubs are concentrated into broad community events celebrating creativity and music. See below for more information.

Candidates Weekend

Every February and March, prospective students travel to the Olin campus for Candidates Weekends, where they engage in build activities, do interviews, and sample Olin's academic and community spirit.

Olin EXPO

The Olin EXPO, held at the end of every semester (December & May), showcases team and individual projects completed during the term.

Fall Open House

Olin hosts Fall Open House annually for prospective students to preview life at the school!

Family Weekend

Every October, the College hosts Family Weekend so that students’ parents and families can visit and explore Olin, its environs, and community.

SLACFest

SLACFest is a community event hosted by the Stay Late And Create (SLAC) group. Anything creative is welcome here, and many workshops and events are hosted at SLACFest to facilitate this.

Concerts

During Fall, Winter, and Spring, many music groups at Olin host concerts where they perform!  Be it OCO, OJO, ORO, PowerChords, or Wired Ensemble, the seasons are alive with the sound of music. 

Description

OCO performs at Candidates Weekends, Family Weekend, Fall Open House every year as well as Olin’s Fall and Spring Expositions (EXPO). In addition, OCO performs Away Concerts that can be hosted at a number of different locales. Previous venues have included the Needham Town Hall (2016), the Great Hall of the Cooper Union in New York City (2017), and the ASEE International Conference in Niagara Falls, NY (2019)

Candidates Weekend

Every February to March, candidates for the Half-Tuition Olin Scholarship travel to the Olin campus for Candidates Weekends. OCO has performed for them since 2003.

Olin EXPO

The Olin EXPO, held at the end of every semester in Dec. & May, respectively, features student team and individual projects. OCO has performed at these showcases since Fall 2002 when the first Expo took place.

Fall Open House

Every October, Olin hosts Fall Open House for prospective students to preview life at the school! OCO has performed for them each time since the first Open House in 2003.

Spring Concert

OCO performs in the Milas Hall Mezzanine on the Olin campus at a Spring event.

Fall Concert

OCO performs in the Milas Hall Mezzanine on the Olin Campus for a Fall event.

Away Performance

Since 2016, OCO has performed at least one Away Concert every year. These events, occurring outside Olin, have been hosted by local, regional, and national institutions.

Description

The Olin Jazz Orchestra (OJO) is the club for those jazz-loving musicians looking to play in a large band at Olin. In addition to the events listed below, OJO has partnered with Olin’s Student Government to stage dance parties, among other events.

Spring Concert

OJO performs on the Olin campus every May at the end of the Spring semester.

Fall Concert

OJO performs on the Olin Campus every December at the end of the Fall semester.

SLACFest

SLACFest is a community event hosted by the Stay Late And Create (SLAC) group. Anything creative is welcome here, and many workshops and events are hosted at SLACFest to facilitate this.

Description

The Olin Rock Orchestra (ORO) traditionally hosts one concert per semester.

Spring Concert

At the end of the Spring semester in May, ORO performs at a concert on Olin campus.

Fall Concert

At the end of the Fall semester in December, ORO performs at a concert on Olin campus.

Description

The Olin PowerChords is the resident a cappella group at Olin. They often work with other a cappella groups to host performances, including the Babson and MIT groups.

Candidates Weekend

Every February to March, candidates for the Half-Tuition Olin Scholarship travel to the Olin campus for Candidates Weekends. PowerChords often performs for them.

Spring Concert

At the end of the Spring Semester in May, the Olin PowerChords perform in the Norden Auditorium in Milas Hall.

Fall Concert

At the end of the Fall Semester in December, the Olin PowerChords perform in the Norden Auditorium in Milas Hall.

Description

In the Wired Ensemble course, students learn about orchestral instruments, including voice, and then write music for those instruments and voices. They engage in multiple performances through the semester, culminating in the Wired Ensemble Event during Olin’s Fall Expo in December. Concerts also occur at Candidates Weekends and special events, both inside and outside Olin.

Candidates Weekends

Every February to March, candidates for the Half-Tuition Olin Scholarship travel to the Olin campus for Candidates Weekends. Wired Ensemble has performed for every Candidates Weekend since starting in 2003.

Class Trip

2019 Wired Ensemble class in front of the Metropolitan Opera House at Lincoln Center in New York where they heard Mozart’s The Marriage of Figaro. 

Wired Ensemble Event

Students in the Wired Ensemble course compose original compositions, all performed at a culminating concert held during Fall Expo in December of each year. The Wired Ensemble has performed at every Fall Expo since starting in 2003.
