Summary

The Olin Rock Orchestra (ORO) is a group of engineering students who set out on the epic quest of fusing the thunderous power of rock music with the mysterious and soothing calm of a classical orchestra. The Olin Rock Orchestra was forged deep in the heart of Olin's campus in a room known as the Jam Room
